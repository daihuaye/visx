@visx/shape

Shapes are the core elements of visx. Most of what you see on the screen, like lines, bars, and areas are all made with shape primitives.

Installation

npm install --save @visx/shape

Examples

Radial Lines
<Shape.LineRadial />
Pies & donuts
<Shape.Pie />
Streamgraph
<Shape.Stack />
Area difference chart
<Threshold />
Stacked Areas
<Shape.AreaStack />

<Shape.BarGroup />
